FAQ

What services does Pets Garden offer?

Pets Garden offers a comprehensive range of pet care services including veterinary consultations, grooming, boarding, training classes, and a specialized pet shop with premium foods, toys, and accessories. Our professional staff is dedicated to providing the highest quality care for your beloved pets.

What are your operating hours?

Our main facility is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Emergency veterinary services are available 24/7 through our hotline. Please note that specific services like grooming and training may have different schedules, so we recommend calling ahead to confirm availability.

Do you offer any membership plans or discounts?

Yes! We offer several membership plans that provide significant savings on regular pet care. Our Premium Paws plan includes monthly grooming, quarterly vet check-ups, and 15% off all store purchases for $49.99/month. For regular customers, we also offer a loyalty program where you earn points with every purchase that can be redeemed for services or products. Senior citizens, military personnel, and rescue pet adopters receive an additional 10% discount on all services.

How does the AI Chat feature work?

Our AI Chat feature is designed to provide immediate answers to your pet care questions 24/7. Simply type your question in the chat box, and our pet care AI assistant will provide guidance based on veterinary-approved information. You can ask about pet behavior, nutrition, common health issues, and general care tips. For urgent matters or specific medical concerns, the AI will direct you to contact our veterinary staff immediately. All chat interactions are saved in your account for future reference.
